How to buy and cook healthy food

Eating healthy on a budget is crucial for overall well-being, providing essential nutrients to support physical and mental health. By prioritizing whole, unprocessed foods like fruits, vegetables, and grains, individuals can create nutritious meals without breaking the bank. Exploring cost-effective recipes and incorporating affordable protein sources, such as beans and lentils, into one's diet can contribute to a balanced and budget-friendly approach to healthy eating. Embracing meal prepping and planning can further optimize resources, ensuring that wholesome, homemade meals are readily available, even for those on a tight budget.
